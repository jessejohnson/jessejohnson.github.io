Who I Am

Everything that matters

Born and raised in Ghana, my life has been coloured by curiosity, bursts of creativity and the occassional dream to do something different.

I'm skeptical about many things, and cynical about most, but I like to keep an open mind and liberal views on life. My interests lie within the arts and sciences, and I spend most of my time thinking. Thinking freely is the most liberating thing a man can do.

Quick Jesse Facts

  • Enjoys quiet moments for reflection
  • Is talkative only when debating history and/or science
  • Is interested in so many things

What I Know

What I Know

A polymath

I grew up wanting to know everything. It has brought me this far, and is sure to see me further. The corollary is, I read an awful lot.

I attended Achimota Senior High, where I developed my knowledge in the sciences, and a strong passion for poetry and literature in general. It was there I learned never to underestimate the power of random reading. Because you may end up needing every bit of knowledge you chance upon.

I had a short stint at Alliance Francaise d'Accra, where I added French to my language portfolio.

KNUST - my university - was a mix of highs and lows, dashed expectations and awesome friendships. I learned a lot there, earning a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in the process.

What I Love

What I Love

The Passion

I am the extreme hobbyist. I love to have hobbies. I cannot live without them.

My many interests keep me busy with stuff I love to do. Besides deep reflection, I work as a freelance fashion photographer, a poet, graphic designer and painter.

Unfortunately, I don't do much painting now.

I'm an active blogger on many different platforms, and style myself as a critic of poetry. I have no right to the title of "novelist"; all endeavours to write my first novel have failed. I only do short stories.
